Teaching & Community Projects

Ongoing initiatives designed to strengthen faith, support families, and build healthy mosque communities in the Gulf and beyond.

Youth & Family

Youth Mentorship & Tarbiyah Circles

Small-group sessions for teenagers and university students, focusing on identity, faith, and real-life challenges.

  • Bi-weekly halaqahs in the mosque
  • Q&A sessions on faith, habits, and character
  • Practical guidance for school & family life
Qur’an & Knowledge

Community Qur’an Circles

Structured gatherings for reciting, reflecting, and living the Qur’an together as a community.

  • Focus on tadabbur and implementation
  • Suitable for different age groups
  • Can be hosted in mosques or homes
Ramadan & Seasons

Ramadan Programs & Iftar Talks

Special talks, nightly reminders, and community iftar programs tailored for Ramadan and blessed seasons.

  • Short pre-Isha or Taraweeh reminders
  • Thematic series for the whole month
  • Custom programs for families & youth
Leadership

Masjid Leadership Workshops

Training for imams, khatibs, and community leaders on how to serve effectively in a modern context.

  • Communication & public speaking
  • Community engagement & conflict resolution
  • Vision, planning, and teamwork

Lecture Series & Programs

Structured series that can be delivered in mosques, Islamic centers, schools, or as online programs.

  • Foundations of Faith: aaqidah, pillars of Islam, and living with certainty.
  • Purification of the Heart: tackling envy, anger, arrogance, and spiritual laziness.
  • Family in Islam: mercy, communication, and raising children with iman.
  • Living Islam in the Modern World: balancing work, social media, and spirituality.
